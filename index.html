Eric J. Schwab, Attorney at Law

Debt Relief with Expertise and Understanding

Eric Schwab is a highly experienced bankruptcy lawyer based in Sacramento, California, and is one of the longest-practicing bankruptcy attorneys in the region, with over 35 years of dedicated service. Since 1990, he has been representing consumer and small business debtors and creditors in bankruptcy liquidation and reorganization throughout the Sacramento Valley. Eric is also the Vice President of the Sacramento Valley Bankruptcy Forum and frequently holds seminars to educate others on bankruptcy law. With a proven track record of helping thousands of clients, Eric specializes in various bankruptcy filings, including Chapter 7, 11, 12, and 13 cases. Known for his compassionate approach and strategic legal expertise, he offers personalized solutions tailored to each client's unique financial situation. Whether you are struggling with overwhelming debt, foreclosure, or creditor harassment, Eric is committed to providing effective, results-driven legal representation, ensuring a fresh start for all his clients.

Our Services

Chapter 7
Bankruptcy

Our Chapter 7 bankruptcy service offers a way to clear certain debts by liquidating nonexempt assets to pay creditors. Eligible individuals and businesses can discharge qualifying debts while keeping exempt property. There's no repayment plan involved, but some debts and liens remain. Let us help you start fresh with a simple and effective solution tailored to your needs.

Chapter 11
Bankruptcy

Our Chapter 11 bankruptcy service helps businesses reorganize and restructure their debts while continuing operations. This option allows companies to negotiate with creditors, modify payment terms, and develop a plan for financial recovery while maintaining operations.

Chapter 12
Bankruptcy

Our Chapter 12 bankruptcy service is specifically designed for family farmers and fishermen, offering a streamlined path to reorganize their debts. This specialized bankruptcy option allows agricultural operations to create a manageable repayment plan while keeping their business running.

Chapter 13
Bankruptcy

Our Chapter 13 bankruptcy service helps individuals with regular income create a 3-5 year repayment plan to manage debts, stop foreclosure, and reschedule certain payments. You'll make payments through a trustee, avoiding direct contact with creditors.

For more information on bankruptcy, visit the U.S. Courts Bankruptcy Resources
