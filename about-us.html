Meet Eric

Eric Schwab is a seasoned bankruptcy attorney and owner at Eric J. Schwab, Attorney at Law, PC, with nearly 30 years of experience. He specializes in providing legal solutions for bankruptcy cases, guiding individuals and businesses through complex financial challenges. Eric earned his J.D. from Lincoln University in Oakland, CA, in 1990 and holds a B.S. in Geography from the University of California, Davis, completed in 1981. His extensive experience and dedication make him a trusted advocate in bankruptcy law, committed to helping clients navigate financial difficulties with professionalism and compassion. He is based in Sacramento, California.

Eric's Role

Eric Schwab is dedicated to helping businesses and individuals seeking bankruptcy liquidation and reorganization. After meeting with clients, Eric presents them with a solution that is crafted based on their personal financial situation. Through Chapter 7, Chapter 11, and Chapter 13 bankruptcies, Eric guides clients through debt liquidation, reorganization, or adjustment. Eric also represents individuals in court hearings, providing legal guidance and representation throughout the legal process. Free consultaions are availabe.
